Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, savory, and a family favorite!

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta or Guanciale
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve some pasta water.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and cheese together, adding a bit of pasta water to create a creamy consistency.
  4. Combine the spaghetti with the pancetta and remove from heat. Quickly stir in the egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve with parsley if desired.

Chicken Tikka Masala

A flavorful dish of marinated chicken in a spiced tomato sauce, served with rice.

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Tikka masala spice mix
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and tikka masala for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic until golden.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice.

Chocolate Cake

A rich and moist chocolate cake that is perfect for any celebration, topped with chocolate ganache.

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 3/4 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, mix dry ingredients. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix until well combined.
  3. Slowly add boiling water and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Cool and frost with chocolate ganache.
